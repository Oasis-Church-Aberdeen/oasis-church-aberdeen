Connect Groups

Home / Connect Groups

Life Is Better Together

Connect Groups are the heart of our church community. These small gatherings meet throughout the week in homes across Aberdeen, providing a space to build genuine friendships, study the Bible together, and support one another through life's ups and downs.

Whether you're new to faith or have been following Jesus for years, there's a group for you. Each group has its own personality, but they all share a commitment to growing together and caring for one another.

Deeper friendships and community
Practical Bible study and discussion
Prayer and support when you need it
A place to belong and be yourself

Join a Connect Group

Ready to take the next step? Fill out the form and we'll help you find the perfect group for you. Our Connect Groups coordinator will be in touch within a few days to answer any questions and help you get connected.

Not sure which group is right for you? No problem! Just let us know your preferences and we'll make a recommendation.

What to Expect

Most groups meet weekly for about 90 minutes. A typical evening includes catching up over tea/coffee, Bible study or discussion, and prayer. Many groups also enjoy social activities together outside of regular meetings.

Questions About Connect Groups?

We're here to help you find your place in our community. Reach out anytime!
