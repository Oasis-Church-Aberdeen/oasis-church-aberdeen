I'm New Here

Partner With Us

Use your gifts to make a difference. There's a place for everyone to serve.

Everyone Has Something to Give

At Oasis Church, we believe that every person has unique gifts, talents, and experiences that can be used to serve others and glorify God. Whether you have an hour a week or can commit to a regular role, there's a place for you on our team.

Serving is one of the best ways to connect with others, grow in your faith, and make a real difference in people's lives. Our volunteers are the heartbeat of everything we do!

Use your unique gifts and talents
Build meaningful relationships
Make a real impact in people's lives
Grow in your own faith journey

Ways to Serve

Explore our volunteer teams and find where you fit best

Worship Team

Musicians, vocalists, and sound/media technicians who lead our congregation in worship each Sunday.

Skills needed: Musical ability, technical skills, heart for worship

Oasis Kids Team

Caring volunteers who teach, lead activities, and create a safe, fun environment for children ages 0-11.

Skills needed: Love for children, patience, creativity (PVG required)

Youth Team

Mentors and leaders who invest in teenagers through Friday youth nights and special events.

Skills needed: Relatability, energy, genuine care for teens (PVG required)

Welcome Team

Friendly faces who greet people at the door, help visitors feel at home, and create a warm atmosphere.

Skills needed: Friendliness, approachability, welcoming spirit

Hospitality Team

Serve refreshments, prepare meals for events, and help create community through food and fellowship.

Skills needed: Hospitality, organisation, food preparation

Tech & Media Team

Run sound, visuals, livestream, and help with website and social media content.

Skills needed: Technical aptitude, attention to detail, willingness to learn

Prayer Team

Pray for the church, individuals, and community needs. Available after services to pray with people.

Skills needed: Heart for prayer, compassion, confidentiality

Setup & Facilities

Help set up and pack down for services and events. Keep our building welcoming and well-maintained.

Skills needed: Practical skills, reliability, teamwork

Community Outreach

Serve our local community through events, food bank support, and practical help for those in need.

Skills needed: Compassion, flexibility, heart for service

Ready to Get Involved?

Fill out the form and we'll connect you with the right team leader. They'll answer your questions and help you get started. No experience necessary â€“ we'll train you!

What Happens Next?

  1. A team leader will contact you within a week
  2. You'll have a chat about the role and your interests
  3. Complete any required training or checks
  4. Start serving and making a difference!

Note: Roles involving children or vulnerable adults require a PVG (Protecting Vulnerable Groups) check. We'll guide you through this process.

Questions About Volunteering?

We're happy to chat about how you can get involved. Reach out anytime!
