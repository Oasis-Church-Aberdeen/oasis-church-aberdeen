I'm New Here

Sundays at Oasis

Join us for worship, community, and inspiration every Sunday

Service Times

Every Sunday

10:30am

Our service typically lasts around 90 minutes and includes contemporary worship, prayer, and a relevant message from the Bible.

What to Expect

Here's what a typical Sunday looks like

Warm Welcome

Our friendly team will greet you at the door and help you find your way around. Grab a coffee and feel at home.

Contemporary Worship

We sing a mix of modern worship songs and timeless hymns led by our worship team.

Relevant Teaching

Our pastors share practical, Bible-based messages that apply to everyday life.

Coffee & Connection

Stay after the service for refreshments and a chance to meet others.

For Kids & Youth

We have engaging, age-appropriate programs running during the main service:

  • Oasis Kids (ages 0-11) - Fun, interactive Bible teaching
  • Oasis Youth (ages 11-18) - Relevant discussions and community

Children are signed in before the service and collected afterwards. All our volunteers are DBS checked.
