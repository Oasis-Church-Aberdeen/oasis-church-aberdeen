Our Commitment to the Community

At Oasis Church, we believe in being a blessing to our neighbourhood. That's why we offer free or heavily subsidised use of our facilities to community groups, charities, and non-profits that serve the people of Bridge of Don and Aberdeen.

Our building isn't just for Sundaysâ€”it's a resource for the whole community. If your organisation is making a positive difference in people's lives, we want to support you.
