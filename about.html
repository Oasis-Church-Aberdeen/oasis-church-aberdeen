Our Story

Oasis Church Aberdeen was founded in 2005 with a simple vision: to create a welcoming community where people from all walks of life could encounter God's love and grow in their faith.

What started as a small gathering of 20 people in a community hall has grown into a vibrant church family of hundreds. Through the years, we've remained committed to our founding principles: authentic worship, biblical teaching, and genuine community.

Today, Oasis Church continues to be a place where lives are transformed, families are strengthened, and hope is found. We believe the best is yet to come as we continue to serve Aberdeen and beyond.

Our Vision

What drives everything we do

Love God

We pursue a deep, authentic relationship with God through worship, prayer, and studying His Word.

Love People

We build genuine relationships and serve our community with compassion and generosity.

Make Disciples

We equip and empower people to share their faith and make a difference in the world.
