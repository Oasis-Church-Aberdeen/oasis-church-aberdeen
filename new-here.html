I'm New Here

You Belong Here

Whether you're exploring faith for the first time or looking for a new church home, we'd love to welcome you.

Plan Your Visit What to Expect

What to Expect

Here's what a typical Sunday looks like at Oasis Church

1. Arrive

Doors open at 10:15am. There's free parking on-site, and you can enter through the main doors where you'll be greeted with a warm welcome.

2. Welcome

Our friendly welcome team will greet you at the door, help you find a seat, and show you where everything is. Just let them know it's your first time!

3. Refreshments

Grab a free tea or coffee before the service starts. It's a great chance to meet people and settle in before we begin.

4. Worship

We start with contemporary worship music—about 20-30 minutes of singing together. Feel free to join in or simply listen and reflect.

5. Message

One of our pastors shares a practical, Bible-based message for about 30-40 minutes. We aim to make it relevant to everyday life.

6. Connect

After the service, stick around for more refreshments and conversation. We'd love to meet you and answer any questions you might have.

Good to Know

A few things that might be helpful before your visit

Kids Welcome

We have programs for all ages. Check in at the welcome desk and we'll take great care of your little ones.

Free Parking

Plenty of free parking available on-site. Just follow the signs when you arrive.

Accessibility

Our building is wheelchair accessible with a hearing loop system available. Just ask if you need assistance.

Refreshments

Free tea and coffee available before and after the service. It's on us!

Service Times & Location

We meet every Sunday at 10:30am. The service typically lasts about 90 minutes, and there's always time to chat afterwards.

Common Questions

Answers to questions first-time visitors often ask

We love kids! We have age-appropriate programs during the service for children from babies through to teenagers. Just check in at the welcome desk when you arrive, and our team will show you where to go. All our volunteers are DBS checked.
