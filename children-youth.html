Children & Youth

A safe place for the next generation to discover faith, build friendships, and have fun.

Growing Together

We believe every child and young person matters to God. Our programs are designed to help them experience His love in age-appropriate, engaging ways.

Ages 0-11

Oasis Kids

Oasis Kids is our vibrant children's ministry where little ones learn about God's love through stories, songs, crafts, and games. We create a fun, safe environment where faith comes alive!

Age Groups

  • Tiny Oasis (0-2 years): Nursery care with loving volunteers
  • Little Oasis (3-5 years): Preschool fun with Bible stories
  • Oasis Explorers (6-8 years): Interactive learning and activities
  • Oasis Adventurers (9-11 years): Deeper faith discussions and service projects

Ages 12-18

Oasis Youth

Oasis Youth is a community where teenagers can explore faith, ask tough questions, and build lasting friendships. We create space for young people to encounter God and discover their purpose.

What We Do

  • Engaging Bible teaching relevant to teenage life
  • Small group discussions and mentoring
  • Games, activities, and social events
  • Worship nights and retreats
  • Community service and mission opportunities

When We Meet

Friday Youth Night: 7:00pm - 9:00pm

Sunday Morning: Youth join the main service or have their own group

Your Child's Safety

We take safeguarding seriously. Here's how we keep your children safe.

Background Checks

All volunteers undergo PVG (Protecting Vulnerable Groups) checks before serving.

Trained Leaders

Our team receives regular safeguarding training and follows strict protocols.

Secure Check-In

Children are signed in and out by parents/guardians using our secure system.

Clear Policies

We follow comprehensive child protection policies in line with Scottish guidelines.

For more information about our safeguarding policies, please contact us.
