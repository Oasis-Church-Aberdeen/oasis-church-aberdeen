Hire Our Facilities

Versatile spaces for your events and gatherings

Your Event, Our Space

Looking for the perfect venue for your next event? Our modern, well-equipped facilities are available for hire. Whether you're planning a wedding reception, birthday party, corporate meeting, or community event, we have spaces to suit your needs.

Located in Bridge of Don with excellent transport links and free parking, our facilities offer a welcoming environment for gatherings of all sizes.

Our Spaces

Choose the perfect space for your event

Main Hall

Up to 150 seated
  • Stage with lighting
  • Professional PA system
  • Projector & screen
  • Kitchen access available
  • Wheelchair accessible

Ideal for: Weddings, parties, conferences, concerts, community events

Meeting Room 1

Up to 20 people
  • Large whiteboard
  • 55" Smart TV
  • High-speed WiFi
  • Tea & coffee facilities

Ideal for: Business meetings, training sessions, small group gatherings

Meeting Room 2

Up to 12 people
  • Boardroom table seating
  • High-speed WiFi
  • Natural lighting
  • Quiet, private space

Ideal for: Board meetings, interviews, counselling sessions, small workshops

Office Space

1-4 people per office
  • Private, lockable offices
  • High-speed WiFi
  • Shared kitchen facilities
  • Flexible rental terms

Ideal for: Small businesses, freelancers, remote workers, start-ups

Commercial Kitchen

Available with hall hire
  • Commercial-grade appliances
  • Large refrigerator
  • Dishwasher
  • Serving hatch to hall

Ideal for: Catered events, cooking classes (with hall hire)

Pricing & Availability

Our pricing is competitive and varies depending on the space, duration, and type of event. We offer discounted rates for:

  • Registered charities and non-profits
  • Regular bookings (weekly/monthly)
  • Community groups serving Bridge of Don

For community groups and charities, please also see our Community Facilities page for subsidised or free use options.
